Alfred Coffee

With multiple locations around Los Angelos, Alfred Coffee is frequented by celerities. Although Alfred is beautifully instagrammable with multiple collabs with popular brands under their belt, including cult-favorite makeup brand Glossier, Alfred is also home to a delicious iced vanilla latté, which they claim to be the best iced vanilla latté you'll ever drink. After tasting it, I'd have to say - they're correct.

Curated café playlists for every mood

Cafés are places not just to get good coffee, but places to study, get work done, meet a friend, etc. For every reason why you might find yourself at a café, we have the perfect playlist for you.

  • Meet Coffeecove's Creator

    Tiffany's coffee addiction started in college. Between assignments and tests, she always found time to grab a cup of coffee to energize her and prepare her for her day. This neccessity for caffeine soon turned into more, as she visited cafés everywhere she went to discover good coffee in cute cafés that made her never want to leave. Named after her Animal Crossing island, Coffeecove is for all of you who also get your energy, literally and figuratively, from a good cup of coffee and a aesthetically pleasing environment.
