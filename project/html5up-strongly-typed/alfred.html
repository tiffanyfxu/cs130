Alfred Coffee have really made a name for themselves in the coffee world. With locations around Los Angeles and recent expasions in Texas, Japan, and Kuwait, Alfred also has many collaborations under their belt. Recently opened "Glossier Alley" with famous makeup brand Glossier has been wildly popular on social media. Their drinks that are unique to "Glossier Alley," including a beautiful lavender latte that's perfect for spring, makes the trip well worth the while. In addition, Alfred's drinks always come with adorable cupholders. Their most recent cupholders are in collaboration with Prime Video and features shows such as The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel and A Very British Scandal.

Alfred is always on my list of must-go coffee shops when I'm in Los Angeles. In fact, it's one of the things I'm most excited about moving to Los Angeles for. Besides the fact that Alfred is incredibly aesthetic and Instagrammable, Alfred also has one of the best iced vanilla lattes I've ever had. In fact, iced vanilla lattes are their signature drink - last time I went, they had a chalkboard sign outside claiming to be "home of the world famous iced vanilla latte." All in all, Alfred is a must try in Los Angeles, and when you go, don't forget to take a pic in front of their famous "But first, Coffee" neon sign!
