A French-inspired restaurant in Los Angeles, Petit Trois is an adorable spot that will transport you straight to Paris with its black and white checkered floor, European cafe-inspired exterior, and French music. It's indoor and outdoor seating makes it a perfect place to enjoy a nice brunch and cup of coffee. All of their dishes are beautifully plated, and even their coffee carries an air of elegance to it, with even their paper straws fitting the Parisian theme so well.

I went to Petit Trois for brunch, and while French food is not necessarily my favorite, their dishes were all so beautiful. For a lover of sweeter coffee like me, though, beware of their coffee - they make it very strong, just like if you were in Europe enjoying a nice cup of espresso. The food, in my opinion, was "meh," but that might just be how I feel about French food in general. Everything was a little bland, and not necessarily cheap. Overall, Petit Trois is an adorable place to enjoy a nice, aesthetic meal and get some beautiful pictures.
