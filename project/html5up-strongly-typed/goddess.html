Goddess an the Baker originally started as a spin-off of Goddess and the Grocer, a popular food and grocery spot in Chicago. This fun café is a fast-casual spot that offers a plethora of adorable baked goods, delicious coffee, and brunch dishes. The decor and interior of the place is absolutely beautiful - every little detail is planned out to perfection.

I LOVE Goddess and the Baker! This is always my go-to spot whenever I find myself in downtown Chicago, and it is the place where I take friends who visit, and they've all loved the place. The case of baked treats you pass by on your way to the cash register is so enticing - you can't help it but to try their sweets! They also have one of the best coffees in Chicago (in my opinion) - I've tried their iced honey wildflower latte as well as their glitter gold latte (with edible gold glitter!) and they were both delicious. I will never stop raving about Goddess and the Baker - if there were one in Evanston, you could probably find me there everyday.

Visit Goddess and the Baker!
