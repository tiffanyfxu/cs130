2d Restaurant (N): An eatery that transports people into their own comic universe using monochrome illusions.

Modeled after a 2d Café in Seoul, South Korea, the 2d restaurant in Chicago is more than just a place to get delicious Korean fried chicken, mochi donuts, and coffee - it's an experience in itself. Everything in the restaurant makes you feel as if you've stepped into the pages of a comic book. With multiple photo locations throughout the place, each guest also gets a polaroid taken for them by the staff.

I visited the 2d restaurant with my mom on mother's day and it was such a memorable experience. We bought tickets beforehad, and the $26 ticket included not only a guaranteed table, but also a meal, a mochi donut, and a beverage, which, in my opinion, was well worth the money. The fried chicken was delicious, as were the mochi donuts. While the classic Phin latte was pretty bitter for my taste, my mom loved her strawberry latte. They also gave her a special, mother's day mochi donut for free! The whole experience was so much fun and so unique, and while I didn't have high hopes for the food at first because the selling point is definitely the 2d aspect, the food was also delicious! If you're thinking of going, make sure you make a reservation and buy the tickets beforehand - thank me later!
